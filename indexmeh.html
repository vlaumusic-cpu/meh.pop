Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I play without internet connection?
A: Yes! Meh Pop is fully functional offline. You don't need an internet connection to enjoy popping bubbles.

Q: How do I enable haptic feedback?
A: Haptic feedback is enabled by default. If you're not feeling vibrations, make sure haptics are turned on in your device settings.

Q: What devices are supported?
A: Meh Pop is compatible with all iOS 14.0+ devices, including iPhone and iPad.
